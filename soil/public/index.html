Our project introduces farming, erosion, deforestation, arts of soil like painting and drawings, analysis of the pictures of the time, and some chemical facts about the soil.

As we saw in the chapter on soil and culture, we learned how south China and the Pearl River Delta region had significant changes to their habitat. Because of the improper practices of clearing land for farming, settlement, erosion, and deforestation changed the region's landscape.
